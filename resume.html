About Me

I am Dr. Vinod Kumar Agrawal, with a distinguished career spanning over 40 years in space research, automotive technology, and academia. I served at ISRO as Group Director, Control Systems, and Project Director (GSAT-4), and later as Chief Technical Officer at GM R&D India. With over 150 publications and multiple prestigious awards, I continue to contribute as faculty and entrepreneur in advanced research areas.

Experience

  • Adjunct Faculty & MSME Owner – Dayananda Sagar University
  • Director R&D & Professor, CSE – Dayananda Sagar University (2018–2020)
  • CTO – Faststream Technologies (2018)
  • Director, Crucible of Research & Innovation – PESIT (2011–2018)
  • LGM, Electrical & Software Architecture – General Motors R&D India (2008–2011)
  • Group Director, Control Systems – ISRO (1978–2008)
  • Project Director – GSAT-4 Satellite Project, ISRO
